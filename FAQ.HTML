Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "Order History" section. You will receive a tracking number via email once your order is shipped.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days for a full refund or exchange.

How do I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team by emailing support@myntraclone.com or calling +1 234 567 890. We are available 24/7 to assist you.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to most countries. Shipping costs and delivery times may vary depending on your location.

Can I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order before it is shipped. Once the order is shipped, you will need to follow the return process.
